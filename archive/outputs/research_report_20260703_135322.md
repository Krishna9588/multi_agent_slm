### Offerings
B2B Data Intelligence platform focusing on technographic and firmographic data, specifically targeting high-growth segments like Hospitality & Travel (PMS, booking engines), Healthcare & Life Sciences (EHR/EMR systems), Real Estate, SaaS & Technology, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Retail, Education, Logistics, Media, Professional Services, Construction, Energy, and others.

### Pricing Structure
Credit-based system where users pay for specific 'reveals' of company data. Free signup includes 200 credits. Pricing tiers: Starter ($49/mo - 1,000 monthly credits), Growth ($199/mo - 5,000 monthly credits + 500 bonus credits), and Enterprise (Custom pricing for high volume, dedicated support, and custom integration). Yearly plans offer a 15% discount.

### Terms And Conditions
Data is verified through a multi-step process (public data collection, quality review, search results are free to browse, 'reveal' uses 5 credits; firmographic data updated monthly, technographics refreshed weekly. They maintain a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for EU customers and adhere to GDPR/CCPA compliance.

### Core Business Activities
Provides AI-powered B2B lead generation, company prospecting, and intelligence gathering. Core activities include scraping and refining high-quality business data, providing niche-specific technical stack signals (technographics), and offering a credit-based model for targeted discovery of contact info and firmographic details.
